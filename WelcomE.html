I am currently a postdoc at the Morningside Center of Mathematics of the Chinese Academy of Science in Beijing.
Formerly I was a postdoc at the Laboratoire de Mathématique Blaise Pascal of the Université Clermont Auvergne.
Before that , I was a teaching assistant at the DMA.

Research interests

My research concerns:
  • p-adic Hodge theory,
  • p-divisible groups and their moduli spaces: in particular Drinfeld's case,
  • the p-adic Langlands program.
